Process

A clear process from first call to launch.

No vague phases and no mess. The whole process stays simple and easy to follow.

01

We get acquainted

In a free call, we listen to your goals and challenges. Together we define what kind of website will best support your business.

02

We prepare a proposal

You receive a clear written plan — what we'll build, what it costs, and when it will be ready. No hidden fees.

03

We build the website

We start work, keep you updated on progress, and incorporate your feedback throughout the process.

04

Review and refinement

You review the result and we refine it until everything matches expectations and you're fully satisfied.

05

Launch and support

Your website goes live, and we remain available for follow-up help and updates — not just during the project.
